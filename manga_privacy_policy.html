Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last Updated: August 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mangali, your ultimate destination for manga discovery and interactive guessing games. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our manga listing and guessing platform.

Quick Summary: We collect minimal data to enhance your manga experience, never sell your personal information, and give you full control over your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Username and email address
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Age verification (to comply with content guidelines)
  • Account preferences and settings

2.2 Manga Activity Data

  • Manga titles you've viewed, rated, or added to lists
  • Reading progress and bookmarks
  • Quiz and guessing game results
  • Comments and reviews you post
  • Favorite genres and tags

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage patterns and site navigation
  • Session duration and frequency of visits

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Personalize manga recommendations
  • Track your reading progress and game scores
  • Enable social features like reviews and discussions
  • Provide customer support

3.2 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and games
  • Optimize website performance
  • Ensure platform security

3.3 Communication

  • Send important account and service updates
  • Notify you of new manga releases (if opted in)
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, and payment processing. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights, or to ensure user safety.

4.3 Public Content

Reviews, comments, and public lists you create are visible to other users as part of the community experience.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality, user authentication, and security.

6.2 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our platform to improve the experience.

6.3 Preference Cookies

Remember your settings, theme choices, and personalization options.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie preference center.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Account Control

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Manage email notification preferences

7.2 Privacy Settings

  • Control who can see your reading lists
  • Manage social features and visibility
  • Opt out of personalized recommendations
  • Adjust data collection preferences

8. Age Restrictions and Parental Guidance

Mangali is designed for users 13 years and older. For users under 18, we recommend parental guidance due to mature content in some manga titles. We provide content rating systems and parental controls to help families make appropriate choices.

9. International Users

Our services are available globally. If you're located outside our primary jurisdiction, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll notify you of significant changes through:

  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Prominent notices on our website
  • In-app notifications

Continued use of our services after changes constitute acceptance of the updated policy.
